How to Signup and login to our system


User register button if you are not a member of our system. You can user Registration button on our nevigation bar to register and after successful register you will redirect to login page and you can login from there or you can youse the login button on the nevigation bar.


How to create a event

After logged into our system you can place a order by clicking on the button Create Event on the nevigation bar. In order to select service providers form there you can refer "Our services" page. There you can find all our service providers and select them by choice.


How to manage your profile


After you logged in you can view your profile by clicking "My profile" on the nevigation bar. There you can see your orders and wether they are accepted or not.
